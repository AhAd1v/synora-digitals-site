About Synora Digitals

An automation company that sells outcomes — time saved, fewer mistakes, more revenue.

Synora Digitals builds custom AI workflows for businesses that are ready to stop doing repetitive work by hand. We’re a new company — founded in 2026 and based in Pakistan, with a second office in Ireland — and we think that’s a strength: you talk directly to the people designing and building your system, and your project is never one of a hundred on someone’s desk.

Our belief is simple: the point was never “AI.” Businesses don’t buy automations — they buy the hours those automations give back.

Why work with us

Custom-built solutions

Built around your existing process and tools — not a template you have to bend your business around.

No-code + AI integrations

We build on proven platforms and add custom AI only where it genuinely pays off — which keeps builds fast and maintainable.

Secure data handling

Least-privilege access, encrypted credentials, nothing shared or reused. The details are public on our Policies page.

Fast deployment

Weeks, not quarters. We keep scopes deliberately small so the first win lands early — then we grow from there.

Ongoing maintenance

Automations are living systems that break when tools change. We monitor and maintain what we build.

Human support

When something needs attention, you reach a person who knows your setup — not ticket #48,291 in somebody’s queue.

Where we are today

We’re early, and we’re honest about it. That means no inflated client counts and no promises we can’t keep — just a limited number of projects at a time, each built with care.
